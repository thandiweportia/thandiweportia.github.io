Our Background.

“Live as if you were to die tomorrow. Learn as if you were to live forever” – Mahatma Gandhi

  • Different Training Services.
  • Sector Education Training Authority (SETAs).
  • Certified Qualification.
  • Disability Awareness and Counseling Services.

With an increase in the demand for educational services in South Africa Magshel consultancy was established to provide training services to close the gap. Through the different sector education training authority (SETAs) the organization decided to offer certified qualification. It was also founded to carter for the needs of people living with a disability by providing counseling, education, general disability awareness and counseling services.

Our Mission

Is providing non-discriminatory quality training, business consultancy and disability awareness.

Our Plan

We want to educate more and more unemployed candidates.

Our Vision

Is transferring skills through training, consultancy and disability awareness.

SETA courses Accreditation and Training Services (ATS).

MagShel is a registered training provider with the Education Training and Development Practices Sector Education and Training Authority (ETDP SETA).

  • Assessor unit standard (ID 115753): conduct outcome-based assessments.
  • Coaching one-to-one training unit standard (id 117877): perform one-to-one training on the job.
  • learner support unit standard (id 117865): assist and support learners to manage their learning experiences. This Skills Programme will be useful to those who provide a basic level of support and assistance to learners about their learning and assessment needs.

Course will equip learners with the required knowledge and skills to coach individual learners in the workplace in their field of expertise. The One-To-One training course will give you a good overview of the National Qualifications Framework (NQF) and will help you to develop your skills and will be a good start in acquiring a variety of qualifications within the fields of Education Training and Development Practices.
